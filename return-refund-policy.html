Return Policy Overview

At S N Battery Sales and Service, we are committed to customer satisfaction. If you receive a product that is not suitable for your vehicle or requirements, you may return it within 7 days of delivery, provided the conditions below are met.

Conditions for Return

  • The product must be unused and uninstalled.
  • The item must be returned in its original packaging along with the invoice.
  • Returns must be requested within 7 days of delivery.
  • Installed or damaged batteries are not eligible for return.

How to Request a Return

Contact our team by phone or email to initiate a return. Please share your order number and reason for the return.
